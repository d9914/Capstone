Introduction to Myers-Briggs

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a personality assessment developed by Katharine Cook Briggs and her daughter Isabel Briggs Myers. The MBTI is designed to categorize individuals into one of 16 personality types based on their preferences in four dichotomies: Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I), Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N), Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F), and Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P).
